8. 행렬 \( A=\left(\begin{array}{rr}a & 2 a^{2}-a \\ 6 & a^{2}-1\end{array}\right) \) 에 대하여 이차정사각행렬 \( B \) 의 \( (i, j) \) 성분 \( b_{i j} \) 가 \( a_{i j}=b_{\ddot{n}}(i, j=1,2) \) 를 만족시킨 다. \( A=B \) 가 성립할 때, 행렬 \( A \) 의 \( (2,2) \) 성분은? (단, \( a>0 \) 이다.) [3.8점]
\[
\begin{array}{l}
2 \quad(2) 3 \quad\left(\begin{array}{ll}
(3) & 4 \\
a_{11} & b_{12} \\
a_{21} & b_{22}
\end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{cc}
a & 2 a^{2}-a \\
6 & a^{2}-1
\end{array}\right) . \\
b_{11}=a \\
b_{12}=a(2 a-1) . \\
b_{21}=6 . \\
b_{22}=\left(\begin{array}{cc}
a & a(2 a-1) \\
6 & (a+1)(a-1)
\end{array} .\right. \\
\left(\begin{array}{ll}
a_{11} & a_{12} \\
a_{21} & a_{22}
\end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{ll}
a_{11} & a_{12} \\
a_{21} & b_{22} \\
b_{12} & b_{22}
\end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{ll}
b_{21} & b_{12} \\
b_{21} & b_{22}
\end{array}\right)=0
\end{array}
\]
