10. 다음 중 옳지 않은 것은?(2.5점)
\[
\begin{array}{l}
\left(\begin{array}{ll}
3 & 5 \\
2 & 0
\end{array}\right)-\left(\begin{array}{cc}
4 & -1 \\
-7 & 3
\end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{cc}
2 a-1 & 6 \\
3 b & -2
\end{array}\right) \\
3\left(\begin{array}{cc}
2 d & -1 \\
-5 & 3
\end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{cc}
12 & -3 \\
-15 & e
\end{array}\right)
\end{array}
\]
(1) \( a=0 \)
(2) \( b=3 \)
(3) \( c=-3 \)
(4) \( d=4 \)
(5) \( e=9 \)
